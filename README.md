# Airgead-Banking-App
Project for CS-210, creating an interest calculator.
README written for journal completion of CS-210.

This program calculates monthly interest based on four instances of user input- Initial Investment Amount; Monthly Deposit; Annual Interest; Number of Years invested.

The greatest challenge I faced was something that wasn't taught in the course, and also wasn't a requirement of the project. The challenge was making the displayed text green. I needed to research on my own outside of the course materials how to accomplish this, in about 10 minutes the text was green.

Nothing else was particularly challenging about this project, adding some tests to make sure the interest is calculated monthly and not yearly were simple additions. Matching the requested formatting exactly was an interesting problem.

To enhance this code further I would like to add further functionality to the program, such as- If you were to withdraw an amount annually after a duration, such as 25k a year after 10 years investing, to be able to see what that does to your overall investment.

For skills that are transferrable to other projects, I am certain one day I will need to make displayed text green again in C++.
To make the product readable and adaptable I followed best coding practices, I initially had far more comments in BankingApp.CPP and the BankingApp.h, I removed most of them later on and likely shouldn't have.
